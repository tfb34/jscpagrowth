About Us

Every company has a story. Here's ours...

Washington J. Sanchez & Associates, LLC is a professional firm dedicated to providing tax preparation, accounting, and consulting services for individuals and small businesses. Since the inception of our firm we have had the pleasure of serving and continuing to learn about our customers. Our goal is to serve the community with integrity, brainpower, and confidence.

Javier Sanchez, Founder

Javier Sanchez, founder and managing partner of Washington J. Sanchez & Associates, LLC, received his Bachelor degree of Science in Accounting from Kean University. He began his career getting experience working along with numerous CPA firms in New Jersey and New York.


As managing partner, Javier is well-versed in solving general accounting issues related with daily business situations. He also has substantial experience in operating rapidly growing medium sized businesses. Javier strives to help his community, and aims to help solve individual as well as business owner financial debts.
