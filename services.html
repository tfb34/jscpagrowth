Services

Working with our clients, not simply for them.

The team at Washington J. Sanchez & Associates offers a diverse array of tax, business and other financial services. We take a proactive approach to understand your needs and goals, and curate the best plan of action for you.

Tax Services

Our experts stay current on changing tax laws and legislation so you don't have to. We will identify key tax-planning opportunities that can minimize your current and future tax liabilities, and help you put these plans in action throughout the year.

Business Services

We aim to help new investors develop accurate techniques to succeed in their business. Our services consist of, but are not limited to IRS/State audit assistance, new businesses formation, dissolutions, legal procedures, acquisitions, loan mitigations and sales.
